App Privacy Policy

Details about the data handling practices of the Café Bella Citta mobile application.

Last updated: 07 Sep 2025

1. Overview

The Café Bella Citta mobile app ("App") is designed to provide menus, location, reservations, announcements, and promotions. This policy explains how the App may collect and process information.

2. Data We May Collect

  • Account info (optional): name, email, phone – if you create an account or make reservations.
  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version, language, country, and anonymized identifiers for analytics and notifications.
  • Usage data: in‑app screens viewed, taps, and interactions to improve performance and UX.
  • Location (optional): coarse or precise location only if you grant permission, used for maps/directions or nearby offers.
  • Crash logs: diagnostics to identify and fix issues.

3. Permissions

  • Location: to show your position on the map and directions to the café.
  • Notifications: to send updates, offers, or reservation reminders. You can disable notifications in system settings.
  • Photos/Camera (optional): if you share photos or scan QR codes for offers. This is only used for that feature.

4. How We Use Data

  • Operate and improve the App and its features.
  • Send service messages (e.g., booking confirmations) and optional marketing with consent.
  • Maintain security, prevent fraud, and measure performance.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers (hosting, analytics, notifications, maps) under contracts that protect your information.

6. Retention & Deletion

We keep data only as long as needed. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting info@bellacitta.ma.

7. Your Choices

  • Control permissions in your device settings (Location, Notifications, Photos/Camera).
  • Opt out of marketing messages at any time.
  • Request access, correction, or deletion via email.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected data from a child, we will delete it.

9. International Transfers

Some services may process data outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date shows the latest version.
