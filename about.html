About Us

Modern chic, warm service, and panoramic Medina views — in the heart of Meknès.

Welcome to Café Bella Citta, your urban escape in the heart of Meknès. Located in the Belle Vue district, just behind the Medina, our café blends modern design, relaxed atmosphere, and stunning views of the old city.

Whether you’re here for your morning coffee, a family outing, or a romantic evening, Café Bella Citta is the place where taste, comfort, and elegance meet.

  • Panoramic Views: Overlooking the historic Medina of Meknès
  • Interior & Exterior: Modern chic indoors + lush outdoor terrace and garden
  • Family-Friendly: Small children’s play area (up to 12 years)
  • Clean & Comfortable: Well-maintained facilities
