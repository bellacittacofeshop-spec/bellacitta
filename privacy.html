Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our app.

Last updated: 07 Sep 2025

1. Who We Are

Café Bella Citta ("we", "our") operates this website and may operate a companion mobile application (the "App"). For questions, contact us at info@bellacitta.ma.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email, phone if you contact us or make a reservation.
  • Usage data: pages visited, clicks, device type, approximate location (via IP) for analytics and performance.
  • Cookies: small files to remember preferences and improve experience.
  • Location data (App): optional, if you allow it for directions or location-based features.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve our website and App.
  • Respond to messages, bookings, or feedback.
  • Measure performance and enhance user experience.
  • Security, fraud prevention, and compliance with law.

4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract: to provide requested services.
  • Legitimate interests: site security, analytics, service improvement.
  • Consent: for optional features like marketing emails or precise location.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, maps) under contracts that require them to protect it.

6. Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to withdraw consent. To exercise rights, contact info@bellacitta.ma.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected data from a child, we will delete it.

9. Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Third-Party Services

  • Maps: We embed Google Maps for location. Google may collect usage data per its policies.
  • Social: Links to Facebook/Instagram may set cookies when you visit their sites.
  • Analytics: If enabled, analytics tools measure usage patterns.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method is 100% secure.

12. International Transfers

Service providers may process data in other countries with appropriate safeguards where required.

13. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest revision.
