1. About this app

Cafe Bella Citta (“we”, “our app”) respects your privacy. The app is designed to work without accounts, ads, analytics, or a backend server. All core features run locally on your device.

What you can do safely:

  • View the menu and categories, search items, and save favorites.
  • See photos, news, and upcoming events.
  • Write local reviews stored only on your device.
  • Request a table reservation by composing a message in WhatsApp or your email app.
  • Optionally add a one-time reminder and open your Calendar app with prefilled details.
  • Open Google Maps to view the café’s location.
Safe wording: we say “view”, “see”, “request”, “compose”, and “open”. We do not claim instant booking, confirmation, tracking, or server storage.

2. Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collectors.

3. Data you provide (handled locally)

  • Reservation details (name, date, time, phone, notes) are used only to create a message you can send yourself via WhatsApp or your email app. We do not receive any messages.
  • Reviews & favorites are stored locally on your device using Room and SharedPreferences. There is no cloud sync.
  • Last-used name & phone may be saved locally to prefill the reservation form for your convenience.

4. Notifications & Calendar

  • The app may create a one-time local reminder for your reservation. On Android 13+, we request notification permission; you can disable notifications anytime in system settings.
  • If you choose “Add to Calendar,” the app opens your Calendar with prefilled event details. We do not read or modify your calendar data.

5. Sharing & third-party apps (user-initiated)

  • WhatsApp and your email app are launched via Android intents only at your request. Any data you share this way is governed by those apps’ privacy policies.
  • Google Maps may be opened to show the café location. The app does not collect or track your location.

6. Backups

Your device or Android system may automatically back up local app data (e.g., favorites, reviews) to your Google account, depending on your device settings. We do not receive or control these backups.

7. Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s personal data.

8. Data retention & control

All stored data remains only on your device. You can delete individual reviews/favorites within the app (where available), or remove all app data anytime by:

  • Settings → Apps → Cafe Bella Citta → Storage → Clear data, or
  • Uninstalling the app.

9. Security

Because we do not operate a backend server, there is no server-side processing and no remote data transfer. Local data stays on your device.

10. Changes

We may update this policy as features evolve. The “Effective date” above will always show the latest version. Updates will be provided inside the app and/or at our official URL.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or privacy requests, contact us at:
support@bellacitta.space
